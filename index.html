My name is Bodun (though I usually go by Edward). I'm a PhD student at UT Austin, working in the UTNS lab with Aditya Akella. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, operating systems, and network. I love building systems for ML and using ML to enhance systems.

Before my PhD, I worked with Christopher Rossbach on heterogeneous computing at SCEA lab, and with Simon Peter on programmable switches.

I have a MS and a BS in Computer Science from UT Austin, where I also worked on GPU benchmarking and enabling OS kernel access to accelerators.

I am happy to collaborate with motivated undergrad and master students at UT. I am also happy to answer questions about my research. If you are interested, please send me an email.
